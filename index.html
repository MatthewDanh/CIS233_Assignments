Matthew "Glitch" Nichols

Who is Glitch?

I am a student at Estrella Mountain Community College, working on an Associate of Applied Science degree in Computer Programming and Systems Analysis. I feel a strong sense of obligation to give back to the community, and I do it by volunteering with and writing for a local LGBT magazine and by supporting Arizona activism groups like Be-A-Hero. As soon as Be-A-Hero received its 501(c)(3) status, I joined the board of directors as the organization's education director. I've worked as a virtual instructor for StudyPool and contributed to a number of Wonder Client Research Reports during the course of my employment.

About Me

As someone who has discovered many of their closest friends and most reliable sources of support via online platforms like Discord and Slack. My interest in the field of Cybersecurity and Data Privacy has grown into a true obsession. It might be difficult for members of marginalized groups to maintain their privacy while engaging in internet activities. As I go towards a career in computer science, I want to contribute to the advancement of programming and cybersecurity so that we may more securely expand our networks of mutual understanding.

Art Projects

Below you will find some of the art that I produced while in ART 112 Two-Dimensional-Design. I took that class thinking that it would be an easy A and it would fulfill a course requirement.

Susanna Battin was my instructor and I had a very unexpected realization because of her class. I love design, and I am a designer. Her class changed my life and objectives as I work through my career path. She taught me how to not only analyze the vessel through which messages and media travel, but also to analyze the perspective from which that content was created.


These are some of the projects that I completed as well as some digital logos that I created for my Discord server.I often try to consider what perspective am I presenting and is this something that the world needs or hasn't seen.
